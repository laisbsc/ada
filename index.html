Say hello to Ada!

Your friendly companion bot.

Hi, I'm Ada!

Nice to meet you.

I was built for those who are not comfortable sharing feelings and real moods with their fellow humans. My aim is to help you overcome loneliness and analyse how you are feeling based on what you tell me.

I was built as a graduation project but beyond that, my mission is to help humans become more aware of their feelings and underlying emotions.

What if you need more help?

Ada can be a helping hand for you to go through a tough moment in a lighter and humorous way.
