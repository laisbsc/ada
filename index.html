About Us

We are the parents of Ada, the friendly chatbot. We decided to build Ada out of the perception that all people feel comfortable sharing feelings and real moods with humans. Ada is a friendly chatbot that wants to help you getting through loneliness.

Ada was built out of necessity, as a graduation project. Beyond that, her mission is to help humans become more aware of their feelings and underlying emotional states. Throughout time, Ada intents to help you perceive how true is the impression of loneliness.

What if you need more help?

Ada can be a helping hand for you to go through a tough moment in a lighter and humorous way.
