Find support

Ada is there to help you but sometimes you need real humans to help you ge through tough bits.

There is no need to do it all on your own. Check out some of our suggestions of where to find a bit of extra help!

  • Samaritans

    Their vision is that fewer people die by suicide. Their mission to alleviate emotional distress and reduce the incidence of suicidal feelings.

  • See Change

    Ireland's organisation dedicated to ending mental health stigma.

  • Pieta

    Pieta provides a free 24 hour support with therapeutic approach to people who are in suicidal distress and those who engage in self-harm.
